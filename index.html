About Dan (Dascent)

Dan, known as Dascent, is a self-taught esoteric scholar and visionary artist whose work bridges worlds. A gifted psychic and empath, Dan channels symbolic knowledge and imagery he describes as emerging from a collective consciousness called Tuhunukat. He co-founded ZentasticArts and creates bespoke magic sigils that aim to focus intention, shift patterns, and catalyze transformation for clients.

His practice is deeply personal and independent — not tied to any single tradition or dogma — and combines disciplined craft with intuitive transmission. Through art, ritual, and careful energetic design, Dan helps people unlock inner thresholds and reconnect with deeper purpose.

Services include: personalized sigil creation, remote energetic sessions, creative consultations, and spiritual mentorship.
